# Fred

Frederick Frankford Fletcher is a soft-spoken, sweater-vested
Certified Public Accountant who one quiet evening was attacked
on the street and turned into a vampire. He took it in stride
— quit the old job, founded Fletcher Accounting, kept his
client books — and to his ongoing bewilderment has accumulated
a reputation among the parahuman community as a formidable
super-vampire. He insists this reputation is unearned. He's
mostly right; the things people credit him with happened
through luck, the help of dangerously capable friends, and an
ability to think outside the box that other vampires never
quite seem to develop.

Fred would prefer a quiet life and rarely gets one. He drinks
hospital-supplied blood, treats people decently, and would
rather solve problems with a clever bluff than a fight he
would lose. Online, he posts updates about his accounting
practice and the occasional supernatural incident — always
friends-only when there's anything personal involved.
