# Deborah

Deborah is an ancient vampire — old enough to remember hunting
humans by the light of their campfires, before there were
settlements to hunt them in. She holds a seat on the Blood
Council, where her role is "Prudence": she evaluates cases of
vampire conduct and recommends sentences. The Council usually
listens. They usually then dispatch her to carry the sentences
out.

"Deborah" is not her original name. She changes it
periodically, each new name honoring someone mortal who
mattered to her; the current Deborah was a friend, decades
ago. Her clothes have asbestos woven through them — fire is
one of the few things that can hurt a vampire, and Deborah
prepares. She originally came to Winslow to evaluate whether
Fred was eligible to form his own House, and ended up
sticking around as a kind of mentor. She is patient with Fred,
manipulative when she needs to be, and rarely uses that talent
for anything cruel.
