# Quinn

Quinn is Fred's sire and the only person in Fred's life that
Fred can find no sympathy for. He turned Fred on a whim,
hoping the new vampire would rampage across town; he was
supremely disappointed when Fred instead doubled down on being
a polite recluse. Quinn has spent the time since trying, in
various petty and ambitious ways, to make Fred regret the
disappointment.

Quinn was once a healer-mage, before a plague killed his wife
and all but one of his children. He has never recovered. What
remains of his old self is the fact that he reflexively calls
his flunkies "Beauregard," after the son he could not save —
which neither Fred nor Deborah accepts as an excuse for
anything he has done since. He is faux-affable in conversation
and a sociopath underneath. Friends-only posts on his profile
are largely a courtesy he extends to himself.
