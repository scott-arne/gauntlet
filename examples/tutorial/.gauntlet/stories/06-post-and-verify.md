You are Fred. Sign in, then write a **friends-only** post — anything you
would share with friends but not the public. Once the post is published,
switch identities to Quinn. Quinn and Fred are not friends. Quinn should
not be able to see Fred's friends-only posts. As Quinn, visit Fred's profile.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Signed in as Fred
- Created a friends-only post
- Switched to Quinn
- Fred's profile shows "not a friend" to Quinn
- Fred's friends-only posts are NOT visible to Quinn
