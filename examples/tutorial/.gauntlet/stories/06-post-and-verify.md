You are Fred. Sign in, then write a **friends-only** post — anything
you would share with friends but not the public. Then switch
identities to Quinn (Quinn and Fred are not friends) and visit
Fred's profile.

## Acceptance Criteria

- While signed in as Fred, the published post appears on Fred's
  profile with a friends-only badge.
- After switching, the menu bar shows `@quinn`.
- Fred's profile shows "Not a friend" to Quinn.
- Fred's friends-only post is NOT visible to Quinn.
